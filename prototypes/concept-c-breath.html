The Art of Simplicity

Good design is as little design as possible. Less, but better — because it concentrates on the essential aspects, and the products are not burdened with non-essentials.

Principles

Innovative, useful, aesthetic, understandable, unobtrusive, honest, long-lasting, thorough, environmentally friendly.

Materials

Every element serves a purpose. Every surface tells a story. Nothing is arbitrary, and nothing remains that does not serve a purpose.
